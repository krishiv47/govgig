GovGig Portal

A government-backed platform for verified gig worker registration, compliance, company allocation, and insurance access.

Core idea Verified workers, regulated access, stronger protections
Prototype focus Registry, companies, compliance, live map, insurance marketplace
GovGig Portal1 / 8

The Problem

For workers

  • No single trusted registry or verified identity layer
  • Weak protection around working hours and unsafe driving
  • Little access to affordable insurance tied to work records
  • Ratings and data usage can feel unfair or opaque

For institutions

  • Companies struggle to source verified workers fast
  • Government lacks clean compliance visibility
  • Insurers lack structured worker data for tailored plans
  • Worker protection systems are fragmented
GovGig Portal2 / 8

Our Solution

Government registry

Verified worker identity, consented data sharing, and anonymous rating preference.

Company demand access

Companies can request workers through a structured government-backed portal.

Insurance marketplace

Insurance providers can publish health plans and workers can buy them in the same portal.

Working Model

Idle state

  • Shows worker location
  • Shows nearby pickup and delivery preview points
  • No active route until assignment happens

Assigned state

  • Company request changes the route
  • Worker marker moves live on the map
  • ETA, location, speed, and fuel change continuously
  • Map reflects live route progress

Key Features

Registry Controls

Worker KYC-style registration, data-sharing preferences, and anonymous ratings.

Compliance Engine

Traffic-aware speed checks, working-hours tracking, and fuel monitoring.

Company Matching

Verified company demand board for allocating registered workers.

Insurance Access

Health insurance plans posted by different companies with in-app purchase flow.
